About Solar Energy

Solar energy is the most abundant energy source on the planet. It is harnessed through solar panels that convert sunlight into electricity.

What are Solar Panels?

Solar panels, also known as photovoltaic (PV) panels, are made up of many solar cells that capture sunlight and convert it into electricity.
